About Me

Hi, I'm Gearoid. (pronounced Gar-road)

Born and raised in the west of Ireland in a small Irish speaking town called An Cheathrú Rua, I am a sports fanatic with a passion for all things nerdy. These include comic books, video games, learning new web-languagues and keeping up to date with what's going on in the world of web development.

I graduated from Dun Laoghaire Institute of Art, Design and Tecnology in 2008 with a BSc in Computing in Multimedia Programming and sensing the approaching financial recession decided to hit the books for another year and achieved a BSc (Honours) in Computing in Multimedia Systems/Web Engineering in 2009.

After University I was employed by visrez for four years as a front end developer/UI designer. I left Visrez in August 2013 to see the world and to experience new cultures.
